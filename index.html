User Guide

Progressive Levels

ProType uses a progressive learning system designed to build muscle memory alphabetically. You start with the most frequent letters and gradually unlock more as you master them.

  • Level 1: Starts with basic keys: e, n, i, a, r.
  • Unlocking: Each subsequent level adds one new character to your practice pool.
  • How to Level Up: Complete 5 sets with at least 95% accuracy and 10+ WPM.

Custom Keys Practice

Want to focus on specific keys? You can switch to custom mode in the settings.

  • Toggle "Custom Keys" in the settings modal.
  • Once enabled, simply click on any key on the virtual keyboard below to lock or unlock it.
  • You must have at least 5 letters unlocked for the generator to work.

Typing Rules

To ensure perfect muscle memory, ProType follows strict rules:

  • If you make a mistake, further typing is blocked until it's fixed.
  • Auto-Fix: You can fix a mistake by simply typing the correct key, or by using Backspace.
  • Words are automatically submitted once you complete the last letter perfectly.
